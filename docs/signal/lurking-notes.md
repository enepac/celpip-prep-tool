# Lurking Notes — Channel 1 (Reddit/Facebook Candidate Discussions)

## Purpose

Passive observation of candidate discussion in public forums. Read only — no posting. Notes feed directly into weekly reviews and inform product decisions.

## Operating instructions

- **Cadence:** 30 minutes × 3 sessions per week, starting day 1
- **Sources:** r/CELPIP, r/canadaexpressentry, r/ImmigrationCanada, plus 2-3 country-specific PR Facebook groups (lock the specific groups during Step 2)
- **Format:** One section per week headed `## Week of YYYY-MM-DD` (use the Monday date). Each entry is a bullet containing:
  - Source thread URL (or "r/CELPIP thread" if linking is impractical)
  - Paraphrased candidate quote (no verbatim copying)
  - Category tag: `frustration` / `tool gap` / `pricing signal` / `outcome anxiety` / `other`
- **What to look for:** recurring pain points, mentions of prep tools, score anxiety, specific task types that come up often, questions about rubric criteria
- **What not to log:** off-topic posts, political discussion, anything not relevant to CELPIP Speaking prep

## Cull check

4 weeks without a product decision changed by anything in this file → review the channel. Either restructure what you're looking for, or kill the channel.
